Water Bending

What is waterbending?

Water bending is one of the four elemental bending arts with the hydrokinetic ability to control water in all of its forms. Water is the element of change, the people of the water tribe are capable of adapting to situations. They have a deep sense of community and love that holds them together through anything. Water benders use slow movements and delicate turns and spin to return the momentum of the opponent’s attack with blasts of water.

Alternative styles of water bending:

  • Blood bending
  • Healing
  • Ice bending

The Moon & Ocean Spirit

The moon and ocean spirit is the source of power in water bending, the original water benders learned to bend by observing how the moon pushed and pulled the tides.
